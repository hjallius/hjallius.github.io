03. Arranging Other People's Flowers

When I was a teenager, I had a blog on Tumblr. It wasn't anything special, and 99.99% of it was reblogged content belonging to others (the rest was comprised of pitiful attempts at very-online humour.)

Really, the whole thing was a gloryfied scrapbook of inside jokes, post-ironic shitposts, and weatherbeaten, dada-esquememes. But

I realise now that what I was actually doing was keeping a commonplace book - shaped humour - still say why are you defending the smell of a ball etc
